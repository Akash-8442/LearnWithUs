Siemens Scholarship Program

Siemens Scholarship Program is a scholarship program conducted by Siemens India. The program offers financial support and employment opportunities to students pursuing a first-year engineering degree at government colleges.

Eligibility:
First-year students of Government Engineering colleges from the following streams can apply:
  • Computer Science /Information Technology
  • Mechanical / Production
  • Electrical
  • Electronics
  • Electronics & Telecommunication
  • Instrumentation
    • Age: Up to 20 years
    SSC: Minimum 60% aggregate
    HSC or equivalent result: Minimum 50% aggregate and minimum 60% PCM aggregate
    Annual family Income: Not more than Rs. 2 lakhs
    Last date for receiving applications: January 10, 2022
    Link for application: www.siemens.co.in/scholarship
